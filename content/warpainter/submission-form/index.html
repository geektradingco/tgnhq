Welcome brave WarPainter to your global directory for commission miniature painters!

Are you a talented miniature painter looking to showcase your work and connect with clients worldwide? The WarPainters, a subsite of Tabletop Gaming HQ, is creating the ultimate global directory for commission miniature painters, and we want you to be part of it!

Why Join The WarPainters?

  • Expand Your Reach: Gain exposure to hobbyists, gamers, and collectors from around the world who are actively seeking skilled miniature painters.
  • Showcase Your Art: Highlight your unique style, portfolio, and specialties to a passionate audience.
  • Simplified Connections: Make it easy for potential clients to find you and inquire about your services.

How It Works

  1. Fill Out the Form: Fill out our quick and easy Google Form. Share your details and portfolio links.
  2. Get Listed: Once approved, your profile will be added to The WarPainters directory.
  3. Connect with Clients: Let your artistry speak for itself as clients from all corners of the globe discover your work.

Who Can Join?

Whether you’re a seasoned professional or just starting out as a commission painter, we welcome painters of all skill levels and specialties. From Warhammer and D&D to historical miniatures and custom dioramas, The WarPainters is here to showcase your craft.
